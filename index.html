Interest and Hobbies

My name is Sachin, and I have a keen interest in data analysis through programming. During my leisure time, I enjoy exploring diverse datasets and experimenting with various programming languages to reveal insights that can be used to solve real-world problems.

In addition to my passion for data analysis, I hold a degree in journalism and have a strong interest in investigative and political journalism. I believe that journalism can be a powerful force for change, particularly in holding those in power accountable and shining a light on critical issues. Investigative journalism involves in-depth reporting and research to uncover hidden or obscured information. By exposing wrongdoing and shining a light on issues that may have gone unnoticed, investigative journalists can drive positive change in society. Political journalism, on the other hand, focuses specifically on reporting and analyzing political events and actions. By reporting on the actions of politicians and government officials, political journalists can inform the public and help ensure transparency and accountability in government. Overall, my interest in investigative and political journalism reflects my passion for using my skills and expertise to make a positive impact in the world.

Moreover, I am fascinated by the intricate connections between celestial bodies and our lives. As an astrology enthusiast, I am particularly interested in the Vedic and Jamini schools of astrology. Astrology helps me gain a deeper understanding of the world around me and its inhabitants. In Vedic astrology, the positions of celestial bodies at the time of a person's birth are used to provide insights into their life path and character traits. Jamini astrology, on the other hand, focuses on planetary patterns and the impact of these patterns on various aspects of life, such as relationships and career.I believe that studying astrology helps me gain a deeper comprehension of the world around me and its inhabitants.

Finally, I am always looking for opportunities to expand my expertise and explore new areas of interest. Whether it's acquiring a new language or pursuing a fresh hobby, I am always excited to take on a new challenge. There's something truly enchanting about sifting through complex data sets and discovering hidden patterns that can influence and reshape the world we live in.
